Gyoza

Ingredients

  • 2-4 packages of gyoza wraps
  • For each bag of wrappers (18 pieces):
    • 120g of ground meat (can be pork, chicken, or beef)
    • 80g of cabbage, finely chopped
    • 15g of scallions (green onions) or Japanese "negi" onion, finely chopped
    • A small piece of ginger (something like 1/2 diameter, 1/4-1/2 inch in length), grated
    • 1/4 tsp salt
    • Pepper (to your taste)
    • 1/2 tsp sake
    • 1/2 tsp soy sauce
    • Sesame oil
    • * Potato starch (to make things stick)

Instructions

  1. Combine the ingredients from the ground meat to the soy sauce in a bowl. If you want to, add a little bit of sesame oil. Mix the contents (with your hands).

  2. If the mixture looks wet or doesn't stick together properly, add a bit of potato starch.

  3. Place just enough filling in each wrapper to fill the center, and fold it in half. Pinch the edges together.

  4. Repeat until filling and/or wrappers are gone.

  5. Oil pan or frier, then fill with gyoza. Remove when wrappers are translucent and sufficiently browned.

  6. For dipping sauce, use whatever you want. I often use a mixture of soy sauce, vinegar, and chili oil, sometimes with ponzu.
