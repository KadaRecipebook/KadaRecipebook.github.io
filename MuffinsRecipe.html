Muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 C flour (my mix is 1/2 C almond meal, 3/4 C gluten free flour, 1/4 C sprouted garbanzo bean flour)
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp cloves
  • 1/3 C sugar (it can be 1/4 C if using sweetened cranberries)
  • 1 C pumpkin puree (Libby's plain one is the best)
  • 1/2 C buttermilk (for dairy free, combine 1/2 Tbsp apple cider vinegar with 1/2 C plant milk)
  • 1/4 C brown sugar
  • 2 TBSP oil (rice bran or grape seed oil recommended; canola would be okay)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 C semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2/3 C dried cranberries OR 1 C fresh and chopped cranberries

Instructions

  1. Combine all the "dry" ingredients in a medium/smallish bowl (from flour to cloves).

  2. Start preheating the oven at 375F.

  3. Combine the "wet" ingredients in a large bowl (from sugar to egg).

  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ones, blend until just combined.

  5. Fold in chocolate chips and cranberries.

  6. Spoon batter into the paper-lined/greased muffin pan. Bake for 25 minutes, or until muffins spring back when you push gently with your finger. Remove from pan immediately, cool on rack until you can't wait any longer ;-)
