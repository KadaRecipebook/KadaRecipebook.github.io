Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 ¼ cups sugar
  • ¾ cup oil
  • 2 cups mashed ripe bananas
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • *Optional - nuts

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients (flour, baking soda, salt, sugar).
  2. Add wet ingredients (oil, eggs, vanilla, mashed banana, optional nuts) and mix well.
  3. Oil pans with shortening (alternatively butter).
  4. Bake at 325℉ for 1 hour.

* To freeze, remove from pans after 10 minutes, cool on racks, wrap and freeze.

* To make 3x recipe, use about 13 very ripe bananas (makes 6 pans).
