Cold Noodle Dish

Ingredients

  • Long noodles (such as pad thai noodles)
  • Dipping sauce
    • vinegar
    • soy sauce
    • sesame oil
  • Eggs (for egg rolls)
  • *Ham (or some other meat: chicken, steak, pork etc.)
  • *Seasoned baked tofu
  • *Cucumber, raw carrots, raw bell peppers (or other vegetables)
  • *all ingredients marked with * are optional toppings, alternatives are fine

Instructions

  1. Boil pot of water. Cook noodles according to instructions on the package.

  2. Drain noodles and let sit.

  3. Make egg rolls:
    1. Beat egg until thoroughly mixed.
    2. Pour enough to cover the bottom of the pan in a very thin layer.
    3. Once cooked through, remove in one piece.
    4. You can cut it in strips or roll it up and cut it into pieces.

  4. Chop up all toppings into long narrow strips.

  5. Serve noodles in bowls, cover in toppings of choice and pour sauce over. Enjoy!
