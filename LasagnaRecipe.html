Lasagna


Ingredients

  • 4 C shredded mozzarella cheese (partially used in cheese mix below)
  • ½ C shredded parmesan cheese (partially used in cheese mix below)
  • Tomato Sauce
    • ½ lb ground beef
    • ½ lb italian sausage
    • 1 onion
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 36-48 oz marinara sauce
    • 2 tbsp tomato paste
    • 1 tsp italian seasoning
  • Cheese Mixture
    • 2 C ricotta cheese
    • ¼ C fresh parsley
    • 1 ½ C mozzarella cheese
    • ¼ C parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Make the tomato sauce.
    1. Brown the beef and sausage in a large pan (or pot).
    2. Mince garlic and onions, and add to meat.
    3. Add seasoning.
    4. Stir to prevent sticking and wait until cooked through. Onions should be somewhat translucent and soft, and meat should be fully brown.
    5. Turn off stove and mix in tomato paste and marinara sauce.

  2. Make the cheese mixture.
    1. Mix together the 2 C ricotta cheese, ¼ C fresh parsley, 1 ½ C mozzarella cheese, and ¼ C parmesan cheese in a large bowl.

  3. Put down a layer of 1 C of tomato sauce.

  4. Cover the layer of sauce with a layer of noodles.

  5. Cover the noodle layer with about ⅓ of the cheese mixture.

  6. Repeat these layers until your pan is almost full or you run out of something.

  7. Finish off the last layer with pasta covered in sauce.

  8. Cover with foil and bake at 350℉ for 45 minutes.

  9. Uncover and sprinkle with remaining cheese (2 ½ C mozzarella and ¼ C parmesan).

  10. Bake for about another 15 minutes or until cheese is browned and bubbly (or broil 2-3 minutes).

  11. Let cool/rest 10-15 minutes.
