Pão de Queijo

Ingredients

  • 2 cups tapioca flour
  • ½ cup + 2 tbsp milk
  • ¼ cup water
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • ¾ cup grated parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 large egg

Instructions

  1. Combine ½ cup + 2 tbsp milk, ¼ cup water, and 3 tbsp oil. Bring to a boil.
  2. Pour over tapioca flour and mix well.
  3. Beat egg and add water if necessary to make the beaten egg reach ¼ cup. Add beaten egg to mixture.
  4. Once incorporated, add cheese a little at a time (1-2 tbsp at a time).
  5. Before shaping the dough, you will probably want to begin preheating the oven to 400℉.
  6. The dough should be gooey/sticky. Wet your hands with cold water or oil to shape the dough into balls, a little larger than golf balls.
  7. Place them on a baking sheet with parchment paper and bake for 15-20 minutes at 400℉ until golden and puffed. Serve warm.

* For freezing, baking sheet of dough balls can be kept frozen for up to 3 months. To bake after freezing, bake at 400℉ for 25-30 minutes instead.
