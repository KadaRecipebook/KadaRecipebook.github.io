Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups flour (the finer the better)
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 lemons total
    • zest (grated peel) of 1 lemon [grate the lemon before you cut it for the juice]
    • juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup rice bran/grape seed oil (canola is okay)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Glaze
    • juice of 1 lemon
    • 1/4 cup sugar

Instructions

* To freeze, remove from pans after 10 minutes, cool on racks, wrap and freeze.

* To make 3x recipe, use about 13 very ripe bananas (makes 6 pans).
