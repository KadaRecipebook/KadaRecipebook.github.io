Lemon Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 C flour (the finer the better)
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 lemons total
    • zest (grated peel) of 1 lemon [grate the lemon before you cut it for the juice]
    • juice of 1 lemon
  • 1/3 C sugar
  • 1/2 C milk
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 C rice bran/grape seed oil (canola is okay)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Glaze
    • juice of 1 lemon
    • 1/4 C sugar

Instructions

  1. Combine the dry ingredients (the first 3) in a small/medium bowl.

  2. Grate lemon in a large bowl, mix with the remaining (sugar through vanilla) ingredients.

  3. Add 1 to 2, pour into prepared loaf pan. Bake for about 40 minutes (in the small oven) or 50 minutes (if using the large oven), until toothpick inserted in the middle comes out clean.

  4. While the bread is baking, combine the ingredients for the glaze in a small saucepan. Stir over medium heat until sugar dissolves.

  5. When the bread is done, take it out of the oven [but keep it in the loaf pan], poke numerous holes with a bamboo skewer or toothpick (careful, the bread is hot). Be sure to pierce ALL THE WAY TO THE BOTTOM. Slowly drizzle hot glaze over bread, basting the top several times. Allow time between bastings for glaze to soak in.

  6. Let cool in pan until glaze is set, about 20 minutes. You can take it out of the pan at this time and cool it on a rack (the original recipe), or leave it in the pan (I never take it out...)
